TweetDeck

Back to Main Page


TweetDeck is a lesser known feature of Twitter that allows you to, among other things, schedule tweets to be posted later, for example, during the tweet storm. This lets you participate in the tweet storm in a limited fashion, even if you can't attend.

Before setting up scheduled tweets with TweetDeck, remember that live participation is always better. If you can make it, please try your hardest to attend! Being a part of this event and truly participating live will be an exciting time for any fan.

The biggest disadvantage to using TweetDeck instead of participating live is that you won't know the correct hashtag to add to your tweets in advance. Your tweets can still mention @Disney but should not contain any hashtags. You should try to mention Eragon and The Inheritance Cycle and our enthusiasm for a new adaptation. For tweet ideas, check out the examples on the main page.
