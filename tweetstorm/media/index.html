Notes for Artists and Contributors Creating New Work

If you want to create new work specifically for the tweet storm, please consider these points.

Your new work can be be pretty much whatever you want from the Inheritance Cycle. However, the best candidates for the tweet storm would be something related to adapting the Inheritance Cycle on screen, possibly referencing Disney, but overall I want to keep it up to you.

If you want to put the hashtag on your artwork, please leave some space for me to add it to the image myself.why? I can use fonts and styles of your choice, which we can talk about together when you send in your artwork.

You can add your website or Twitter handle or any other signature to one of the corners - in fact, I'd encourage it! People will be tweeting and retweeting it all over Twitter, so it would be great to be able to link your artwork back to you.

To ask questions or submit work, visit our Discord server here. To ask questions and submit work privately, message me on Discord at Hellomynameis99#9999, on Twitter at @MCAlagaesia or on Reddit at u/MCAlagaesia.

If you want to contribute something other than artwork, please message me! I'd love to hear your ideas.
