About

We're touring MCAlagaësia with Christopher Paolini!

During the tour, we will focus on locations that have been built or updated since last year's tour.

Can I join?

Just like last time, members of the public will be able to join supporters and staff on the MCAlagaësia server for the duration of the tour. You will not need to support the server to connect. To join us, connect to play.mcalagaesia.com.

While we are doing our best to prepare for any eventuality, this may be subject to a limitations if the server becomes overloaded due to higher than expected demand.

If this happens, the server will attempt to give priority to supporters due to the "Reserved Slot on All Servers" Premium mechanic.

What if I can't make it?

The tour will be live streamed on the MCAlagaësia YouTube channel. You will be able to watch the archived stream after the event.

Christopher has expressed interest in recording, editing and uploading his perspective at a later date. This is subject to several external factors, and we will attempt to provide more information closer to the date.
