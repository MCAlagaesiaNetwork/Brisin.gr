About

We're hosting a weekly read-along of the Inheritance Cycle.

Join us in the Reading Stage on the Arcaena & MCAlagaësia Discord Server when the countdown hits zero.

Each session begins with 30-45 minutes of reading, and then opens up to discussion from all participants.

Become a Reader

Any member can volunteer as a reader.

When reading, you can choose to read as much as you want, from just a few pages to an entire chapter.

To volunteer, use the "Request to Speak" button during a session, or make yourself known to a stage moderator prior to the event.
